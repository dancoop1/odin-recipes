Turkey Club

Description

This is a recipe for a delicicious Turkey Club sandwich!

This sandwich is simple, fast, and nutritious.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bacon
  • 3 slices bread, toasted
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 leaves lettuce
  • 2 (1 ounce) slices cooked turkey
  • 2 slices tomato

Directions

  1. Cook bacon in skillet over medium, high heat until desired state.

  2. Spread mayonnaise on each slice of bread.

  3. Place turkey and lettuce on a slice of bread.

    Add second slice of bread on top, cover with tomato, bacon and final slice of bread.

  4. Enjoy!
