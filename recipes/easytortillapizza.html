Easy Tortilla Pizza

Description

This is a recipe for easy Tortilla Pizza!

Fast and simple. Makes a great snack!

Ingredients

  • 1 Tortilla
  • Olive oil
  • Black Pepper, Garlic Powder, Dried Oregano
  • Tomato sauce
  • Toppings: sliced chicken breast, green bell peppers, green onions
  • Mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. Brush the tortilla with olive oil and season it. Bake at 350 for a few minutes.
  2. Top with tomato sauce and toppings and sprinkle liberally with cheese.
  3. Bake again until cheese is melted. Sprinkle oregano.
  4. Enjoy!
