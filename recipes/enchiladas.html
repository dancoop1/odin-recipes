Enchiladas

Description

This is a recipe for a dish of delicious Enchiladas!

These are green enchiladas which can be made in less than an hour.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Monterey Jack Cheese
  • 1 Cup shredded Mild Cheddar Cheese
  • 12 Corn Tortillas
  • 2 Cups shredded, cooked Chicken breast
  • 1 Cup chopped Green Chile
  • 2 Tbsp Butter
  • Garlic, Salt, Cumin
  • Cooking oil
  • 1.5 Cups of Chicken Broth

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

  2. Mix Monterey and Cheddar cheeses together.

  3. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add Broth. Add green chiles, garlic, salt, and cumin.

    Simmer for 15 minutes. Remove From heat.

  4. Heat oil in skillet over medium heat. Fry tortillas 10-20 seconds per side. Dry on paper towels.

  5. Place two tablespoons of chicken and two tablespoons of cheese down the center of each tortilla.

    Roll the tortilla and place seam side down in a baking dish. Repeat for each tortilla.

  6. Cover as needed with green chile sauce. Sprinkle remaining cheese.

  7. Bake in oven for twenty minutes, or until cheese is melted.
